Steps of Booking

  1. Go to LiveWIRE Brunei E-Suite website at http://livewirebrunei.com/esuite.
  2. Click on Book Now or Book Your Seat button to proceed on Booking Section
  3. Fill in the required information
  4. Check seats availability.
  5. Choose your desire date and seat number.
  6. Click on Submit button.
  7. Successful bookings will get a confirmation email and will need to follow further instructions for payment of booking.
  8. On the day of booking, LiveWIRE Staff will need to see your payment receipt for confirmation to grant access to E-Suite.

    9. You may now use the desk you have booked at E-suite at the allocated time.
